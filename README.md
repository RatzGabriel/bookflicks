## About The Project

[![Product Name Screen Shot][product-screenshot]](https://ibb.co/ZTd1yGF)

<a href="https://master--resilient-kitsune-c2ec5a.netlify.app/">View Demo</a>

Are you tired of spending hours searching for your next favorite book? Look no further than our website, built with React and powered by Google's book API. With our easy-to-use search function, you can find books and authors in just seconds. And once you've found a book you love, you can add it to your favorites list and keep track of all your must-reads.

Our website was designed with the user in mind. We know that your time is valuable, so we've made it easy to search for books and organize your favorites all in one place. Plus, with React's lightning-fast performance, you'll never have to wait for pages to load or searches to run.

So why waste your time scouring the internet for your next great read? With our website, you can find and organize your favorite books quickly and easily. And with our commitment to always improving and adding new features, you can be sure that our website will be the last book search tool you'll ever need.

Use the `BLANK_README.md` to get started.
